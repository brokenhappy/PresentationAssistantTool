Imprass — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Overview

Imprass is a presentation remote control app. It connects your phone to your desktop computer over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) so you can control your presentations wirelessly. Your privacy matters — Imprass is designed to work entirely offline with no data sent to any server.

What data is stored

  • Device identifier: A randomly generated UUID stored locally on your device. This ID allows the desktop app to recognize your phone when reconnecting. It is never transmitted over the internet.
  • Paired device list: The app remembers which desktop computers it has connected to, stored locally on your device.

What data is NOT collected

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Bluetooth

Imprass uses Bluetooth Low Energy to communicate with the desktop companion app. Bluetooth data is exchanged directly between your phone and your computer — no data passes through any server or cloud service.

Data retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Third-party sharing

Imprass does not share any data with third parties.

Children's privacy

Imprass does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
